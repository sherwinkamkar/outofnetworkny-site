About the Firm

Experienced New York Attorneys. Provider-Focused OON Practice.

Madison Law Firm PLLC is a New York-based law firm with a practice that spans personal injury, consumer protection, lemon law, rideshare litigation, and — increasingly — out-of-network reimbursement disputes for medical providers.

Our OON practice was built in response to a clear pattern: New York's out-of-network laws give providers meaningful legal rights to dispute inadequate payments, but most practices don't have the legal infrastructure to act on those rights. Insurance companies know this and systematically underpay claims at scale.

We represent OON providers on a contingency basis — no recovery, no fee — in both New York state IDR proceedings under Financial Services Law Article 6 (and Insurance Law § 3241) and federal No Surprises Act arbitration under 42 U.S.C. § 300gg-111. Our focus is on physicians, surgeons, anesthesiologists, ambulatory surgical centers, and medical practices that bill out-of-network in New York.

We are a small, focused firm. That means you get direct attorney attention on your matter, not a handoff to a paralegal. When you call our number, you reach someone who knows your case.
